Introduction

Gasnowwallet is a wallet payment solution for clean cooking and mobility sector using Near Field Communication, QR, USSD, card payment solution that is turning mobile phones into a more secured payment gateway for easy access of essential service for Mass market demography using card payment, USDD, QR, Near Field communication technology to facilitate direct to customer (B2C) approach for housholds and individuals to access cleaner energy for cooking (LPG gas) and for powering mobility in the transport sector.

About Us

Gasnow wallet is a tech startup company that is digitizing access to cleaner cook fuel (LPG) and CNG autogas energy in Nigeria and Africa.

With access to over 3million real-time banking data and over 30k registered user base data of energy LPG retail centres and LPG kioks in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South-Africa. We provide B2B and B2C seamless digital ease and access to buy now pay later(BNPL) that is web/mobile enhance. Gasnow wallet is seamlessly powering LPG B2B/B2C consumers with microcredit, payment and financial inclusion services for LPG last mile consumers.
